Create a Webapp for website which will be used to track the public speaking capabilities, oratory skills, speaking, confidence of students.
1. Admin can add students, students can give public speaking events, other students will rate them. 
The events will happen in person so admin will give attendence to students who have given the event.
2. Each student will have a profile page which will show public speaking strenghts of student, each student will login with phonenumber or google account.
3. Admin can add public speaking events
4. Events will be of 3 types:
    a. Speed Talks ( 1 min ). Rating criteria will be stars out of 5
    b. Long speech (  5 min - 15 min ).Ratina critera will be different here, it will be mean of these items:
        i. Clarity of speech
        ii. Confidence of speaker
        iii. Body language
        iv. Presentation skills
        v. Content of speech
    c. Debate ( 10 min - 20 min ). Rating criteria will be different here, it will be mean of these items:
        i. Clarity of speech
        ii. Confidence of speaker
        iii. Body language
        iv. Presentation skills
        v. Content of speech
5. The webpages should look neat & clear & easy to navigate.
